Coming soon - Aristotle Enterprise Community!

Soon we will be launching a suite of high-security, high-availability enterprise libraries to meet the specialised needs of large organisations funding an a expert-driven board to help build a sustainable future.

Securing our future. Together.

We understand that enterprise customers need enterprise solutions. Thats why in the coming months we will be beginning the next phase in our goal of sustainable open source development.

The Aristotle Enterprise Community will be formed from a series of working groups and development teams working on enterpise level extensions to the core functionality of the Aristotle Metadata Registry. These include advanced editing capability, secure user access controls, integration with data platforms, semantic metadata analysis tools and more.

By working together with our enterprise clients we will build a community of engaged experts to help guide Aristotle as we grow.
