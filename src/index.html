Aristotle Metadata Registry
makes data make sense.

The Aristotle Metadata Registry is a modern, user-friendly collaborative tool for recording business knowledge about data.

Our Registry captures human intelligence about data so it can be integrated with business intelligence solutions, and can be deployed in the cloud or as an enterprise on-premise solution.

We support you by delivering secure cloud-hosted registries, metadata goverance consulting, custom software integrations, and training and support.
