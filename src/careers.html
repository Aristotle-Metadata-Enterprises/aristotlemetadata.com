Careers

Join us and do work that makes an impact!

Aristotle Cloud Services Australia is the company behind the Aristotle Metadata Registry, a platform that helps government and academic institutes provide positive outcomes for people through improved data management systems.

We currently work with a growing number of government agencies and businesses in Australia and growing our team so we can serve more customers both here in Australia and around the world.

And we want you to join us!

Join in and help make history!

Benefits of working with us include:

  • Competitive pay and equity packages based on the work you do here and not your previous salary
  • Casual work environment in a co-working space shared with some of Canberras top startups
  • Opportunity to define your role and responsibilities as the company grows
  • Company supplied laptop to support flexible working arrangements
  • Opportunities and encouragement to attend conferences and networking events

Current positions

Full-stack developer

Role: You will work in our team building new features in our Django-based content management system that will be used by hundreds of users across a range of government and professional sectors. You will build new user interfaces based on client feedback and work with our team to test and deploy these on a rotuine basis.

To be successful you will need:

  • Experience with frontend web-development using JavaScript
  • Experience with backend web-development
  • Proficient working with CSS, HTML, web development
  • Permission to work in Australia

It would be useful if you had:

  • A relevant Certificate, tertiary qualification (such as Computer Science or Software Engineering), or equivalent experience.
  • Javascript: Experience with modern Javascript libraries such as Vue.js, NodeJS, or Vis.js
  • Experience with backend web-development using Python with frameworks such as Django or Flask
  • Experience with Docker and knowledge of modern container architecture

Migration and Integration developer

Role: You will work in our team building and maintaining custom code to help clients integrate their software with the Aristotle Metadata Registry APIs. Your code will read from and write to a variety of formats, including other RESTful APIS, JSON document stores, XML files, MS Office formats and plain text records.

To be successful you will need:

  • Experience with querying GraphQL and RESTful API using scripting languages
  • Experience using scripting languages to perform database queries and transformations
  • Experience with backend web-development
  • Permission to work in Australia

It would be useful if you had:

  • A relevant Certificate, tertiary qualification (such as Computer Science or Software Engineering), or equivalent experience.
  • Experience with XML and related techologies
  • Experience with Docker and knowledge of modern container architecture
  • Experience deploying to serverless or Function-as-a-Service environments such as AWS Lambda
  • Experience with backend web-development using Python with frameworks such as Django or Flask
